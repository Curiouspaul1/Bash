About Bammy College

     

Bammy-Collge High School ranks among the top of high schools in the state and offers students a comprehensive 9-12 grade education. Students have more than 20 courses to choose from including practical/contemporary art, to pure sciences and commercial subjects compulsory for an O'level degree, as they progres down their academic paths. The school offers students several opportunities in performing and fine arts, as well as an avenue for each tribe amongst students to showcase the endearing sides of their culture , as they observe the well-schemed out plan for 'Cultural Day'.
